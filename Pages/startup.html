Innovative startups incubated under AIC-SOA Foundation

Our Incubated Startups

PLANTERY

Plantery has developed innovative plant-based energy storage systems using grass as a raw material, eliminating toxic chemicals commonly used in batteries. The startup has created more than 15 prototypes, partnered with Indian Oil (₹29.5 lakh grant), attracted Shell Global USA interest, won over 14 awards, gained IIT support, and ranks among India’s top green solution startups.

driEV

DriEV is a dynamic venture fusing innovation, sustainability, and local manufacturing to reshape the future of campus and urban commuting. The startup focuses on electric mobility solutions and has been showcased at ICAI-Startup Sphere’23 and exhibited at Aarogya Manthan.

EMINENT PRODUCTS

Eminent Products focuses on developing compact, high-torque gearboxes with precision power transmission. The technology addresses the rapidly growing automation market across defense, robotics, electric vehicles (EVs), AGVs, and medical devices.

PHOTOSPIMEDX PVT. LTD.

PhotoSpimeDx is developing a smartphone-based fluorescence spectroscopic device for early-stage cervical and oral cancer diagnosis. The solution combines offline and online market research to deliver affordable, sensitive, user-friendly, and equipment-free diagnostic products.

EVSPEER

EVSPEER works on EV charging infrastructure and energy storage solutions. Its products include Charz (static EV level-1 charging unit) and Anerzy (portable EV charger with battery backup). The company has sold over 20 Charz units and gained 438 subscribers for Anerzy.

GREEN MANDI DEALS PVT. LTD.

Green Mandi is building a tech-driven ERP platform that connects farmers, traders, SMEs, and buyers through a secure unified B2B marketplace. The solution aims to digitize the unorganized agri-trade sector with transparency and trust.

SPK-MAK TECHNOLOGIES

SPK-MAK Technologies Pvt. Ltd. offers end-to-end electronic product engineering and ODM solutions. The company integrates Robotics, Embedded Systems, IoT, AI/ML, and CAD to transform ideas into market-ready, cost-effective products.

HYDROVALLEY

Hydro Valley is transforming sustainable agriculture through indoor hydroponic farming. Based in Bhubaneswar, it produces pesticide-free, nutrient-rich food using RO-purified water and controlled environments, minimizing water usage and carbon footprint.

ANGIOSENSE

AngioSense develops a non-invasive diagnostic device to detect blood vessel blockages without radiation or contrast dyes. Using sensors for SpO₂, temperature, pulse, and bioimpedance, it enables faster and safer vascular health assessment.

AXHA BIOENERGY PRIVATE LIMITED

AXHA Bioenergy operates in the renewable energy sector, manufacturing eco-friendly biodiesel using diverse feedstocks including used cooking oil and non-edible oils. Its facilities support scalable and sustainable fuel production.

STUDY TOURS INDIA

Study Tours India delivers experiential learning through industrial tours, cultural trips, awareness campaigns, and focused workshops. The startup aims to develop practical, creative, and career-oriented skills among students.

URBAN HYPHAE

Urban Hyphae is revolutionizing fashion with sustainable, mushroom-based leather alternatives. The startup combines eco-conscious design with cruelty-free materials to reduce environmental impact while maintaining durability and style.

POWERBOX PVT. LTD.

PowerBox offers Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) solutions that make energy storage affordable and flexible. Users can subscribe to solar systems and EV batteries while reducing upfront costs and ensuring hassle-free maintenance.

SMART BLOOM

Smart Bloom automates flower pollination using robotics, machine learning, and agricultural science. Built on open-source platforms, the system enhances yield, efficiency, and precision in controlled farming environments.

PARQ PVT. LTD.

ParQ is a smart parking platform that connects drivers with real-time bookable parking spots through mobile and in-car integration. It optimizes urban mobility while reducing fuel consumption and congestion.

HEALTHLIGENCE

Healthligence is building a patient-centered EHR system using AI-driven interactions and LLM integration. The platform enables efficient clinical workflows, real-time collaboration, and secure cloud-native healthcare data management.

FRIDAY EVENTS PVT. LTD. – EINIVIE

EINIVIE simplifies event planning by connecting users with verified vendors through a standardized digital platform. The solution offers e-invitations, guest management, live updates, and ticketing for private and public events.

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA)

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA), a Deemed to be University under UGC, was established in 1995 in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The university is committed to academic excellence, research innovation and holistic development across multiple professional disciplines.

AIC-SOA FOUNDATION

AIC SOA Foundation is an Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA) Deemed to be University, supported by NITI Aayog.It promotes innovation and entrepreneurship by incubating startups, providing mentorship, infrastructure,industry connections,and training to transform ideas into scalable ,impactful enterprises for students ,researchers ,and early stage founders acrsoss diverse sectors.
