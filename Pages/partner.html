Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA)

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA), a Deemed to be University under UGC, was established in 1995 in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The university is committed to academic excellence, research innovation and holistic development across multiple professional disciplines.

AIC-SOA FOUNDATION

AIC SOA Foundation is an Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA) Deemed to be University, supported by NITI Aayog.It promotes innovation and entrepreneurship by incubating startups, providing mentorship, infrastructure,industry connections,and training to transform ideas into scalable ,impactful enterprises for students ,researchers ,and early stage founders acrsoss diverse sectors.
