Atal Innovation Mission

The Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), launched by the Government of India in 2016 under NITI Aayog, aims to foster innovation and entrepreneurship nationwide. As part of this mission, the Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) initiative supports technology-driven startups across the country.

Focus Areas

Med-Tech

Advancing medical technology for better diagnostics and patient care. Supporting innovations in devices, imaging, and healthcare delivery.

Health-Tech

Empowering health solutions through digital platforms and AI. Focusing on telemedicine, health apps, and smart monitoring.

Digital Health

Integrating technology for seamless healthcare experiences. Promoting electronic health records and remote consultations.

Social Innovations

Driving positive change in society through sustainable solutions. Empowering communities with clean-tech, agri-tech, and climate action.

Core Industries

Start-ups Working in IoT, AI, and Data Analytic, Industry 5.0, Industry 6.0.

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA)

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA), a Deemed to be University under UGC, was established in 1995 in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The university is committed to academic excellence, research innovation and holistic development across multiple professional disciplines.

AIC-SOA FOUNDATION

AIC SOA Foundation is an Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (SOA) Deemed to be University, supported by NITI Aayog.It promotes innovation and entrepreneurship by incubating startups, providing mentorship, infrastructure,industry connections,and training to transform ideas into scalable ,impactful enterprises for students ,researchers ,and early stage founders acrsoss diverse sectors.
